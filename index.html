FitHIT

Hipsterize your fitness

FitHit is a sleek and easy to use interval timer and workout logger for athletes in training. It is perfect for CrossFit, HIIT, tabata and more. Just create your workout and press Start, or choose from our 5 built in workouts. Clever large display and color-driven UI means less time fiddling with apps, and more time to focus on your workout.

Large display, awesome colors

Designed for gym use. FitHIT's workout screen uses large fonts to display current exercise information and assigns different screen colours to each exercise, so you know when it's time to change exercise without interrupting your workout.

Easy to use during workout!

Almost no navigation required during the workout! FitHIT lets you concentrate on your workout. Either switch automatically to the next exercise, or double tap anywhere to start the next one.

Build any workout

Create your own workouts, by selecting a number of sets or as many reps sets as possible for a fixed time. You can set exercises by number of reps, time or distance too. FitHIT comes with 5 built-in workouts: Fight Gone Bad, Cindy, Filthy 50, Fran and Helen.

Track your progress

FitHIT logs all your workout activity, with both high level and detailed workout logs available.

Set multiple alerts

FitHIT can notify you about your next exercise by vibrating, sound alerts or even tell you what to do next.

Optimized play controls

Castro reimagines how play controls should work on iPhone. The compact controls make room for content and the revolutionary scrubber stays out of your way until you need it.
