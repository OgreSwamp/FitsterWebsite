FitHIT

Personalize Your Workout

FitHIT is a sleek and easy to use interval timer and workout logger for athletes in training. It is perfect for CrossFit, HIIT, tabata and more. Just create your workout and press Start, or choose from our 5 built in workouts. Clever large display and color-driven visual timer means less time fiddling with app, and more time to focus on your workout.

Large font, bright colors!

Designed for gym use. FitHIT's workout screen uses large fonts to display current exercise information and assigns different screen colours to each exercise, so you know when it's time to change exercise without interrupting your workout.

Focus on workout

Almost no navigation required! FitHIT lets you concentrate on your workout. Either switch automatically to the next exercise, or double tap anywhere on the screen to start the next one. Clear visual cues keep you updated on progress and lets you know what's up next.

Build any workout

Create your own workouts, by selecting a number of sets or as many reps sets as possible for a fixed time. You can set exercises by number of reps, time or distance too. FitHIT comes with 5 built-in workouts: Fight Gone Bad, Cindy, Filthy 50, Fran and Helen.

Track your progress

FitHIT logs all your workout activity, with both high level and detailed workout logs available.

Multiple alert options

FitHIT can notify you about your next exercise by vibration, sound alerts or even by voice instruction. The choice is yours.

Easy to use

If your workout is interrupted, then it's really easy to pause and resume exactly where you left off, without losing your place.
